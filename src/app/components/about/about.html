About Bespoke Spaces

Crafting Practical Luxury

Spaces designed for modern high-rise living

Bespoke Spaces is a boutique interior design studio based in Kharadi, Pune. We design luxury, high-performance homes for discerning apartment owners. From bare shells to turnkey master designs, we focus on spatial optimization, exquisite materials, and modular excellence.

Our active presence is highly visible across Pune's premium towers, including Shubh Nirvana in Viman Nagar, Godrej Boulevard and Majestique Towers in Kharadi. We handle all elements including false ceilings, civil works, custom storage paneling, modular kitchen assemblies, and final interior styling.
